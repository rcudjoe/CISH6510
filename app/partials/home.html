The Party Begins Here

This site offers, at first look, the histories and compositions of all the categories of alcoholic drinks: spirits, liqueurs, wine beer and cider. We also look at what equipment is needed and what techniques should be learned in home bartending. Whether you are throwing a cocktail party, or whether you just want to know how to make cocktails are good as - if not better than - those you have tasted in the cocktail bar, all the information you need can be found right here.

Finally, getting down to business, this site provides several cocktail recipes, arranged by type, so that you can happily expand your repertoire. The drink after work or on Saturday night no longer be ther same old bottle of beer, glass of wine. The drinking world is your oyster. Do it wisely and have fun.
